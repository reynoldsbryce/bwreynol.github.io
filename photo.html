In our AENG 110 class one of our projects was a digital photography project, where we had to take pictures using proper techniques, and then edit them. I used a variety of pictures and different techniques. I used centering for the TOYOTA RAV4 and Gidget, framing for The Grand Canyon, and the rule of thirds for The Creamery. To edit my photos I used photoshop, editing the coloration as well as taking out some blemishes in a few of the photos.
