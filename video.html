Video Link

Our final project was a videography project, where we had to form groups and design something to make a video and advertise for. Our group initially chose to make a video about a club that we are all in, the Technology & Engineering Education Collegiate Association (TEECA). After making an initial script, we decided to make our video about TEECA East, a conference that the club went to the weekend before our video project was due.

We shot video of various activities that were going on at the conference, and then voiced over it all giving a brief description of everything. All of which was done using an iPhone.
